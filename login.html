Minecraft Session Login

 

What is this?

You can use this form to login to the Minecraft central server at login.minecraft.net, just as you do whenever you start the game. Your username and password are sent directly from your web browser to Mojang's server, over a secure connection (https), and nowhere else.

After you successfully login, you will see something like this: 

1281688214000:a348b469d915a40c9eebd2b9919c1a39:your_username:8204407531530365141

                                                             ^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
                                                               your session id
The last number is your session ID, which you use to join servers. You get a new one every time you login.
